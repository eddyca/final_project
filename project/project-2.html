Materializing Facebook

Curious to see what it's like to social network without technology, I used the mail system to send pictures and updates to friends and family. I chronicled this experience and made a book. I juxtaposed snippets of my journal and images with the hand written replies I received.

Date: 2011

Materials: One color screen printed hard cover accordion book
