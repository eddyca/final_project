Berlin Street Art 2008

I was unaware of Berlin's street art history until I visited for a 2 week study abroad trip. I became interested in documenting this phenomonen with my camera. As I photographed and researched, I learned that sreet artists and graffiti painters from around the globe utilize its buildings as a canvas, creating a uniquely colorful landscape.
