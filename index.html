About

I have the ability create and translate a design direction across multiple platforms and media (print, online, interactive, exhibition installation) thanks to my several years of experience working for the Exhibitions Department at The Field Museum. I'm in the process of bringing my print design skills to the web by shifting my focus to front end web development. In my free time you'll find me in Sublime text honing my skills with HTML, [Sass flavored] CSS and jQuery.
